Solar Charge Controllers

Charge controllers are integral to any solar system: they take the uncertain voltage from a solar panel and condition it to safely charge batteries, prolonging battery life. We stock both PWM and MPPT controllers.

Phocos · PWM

CXN Series

Programmable PWM controllers with an LCD display and temperature compensation, auto-selecting 12V or 24V. Pairs with the optional CXM remote display.

10 – 40A 12V / 24V auto LCD display Temp. compensation
Phocos · PWM

CML Series

Mid-range PWM controllers with three-stage charging and an LED display, auto-selecting 12V or 24V. A nightlight (CML-NL) variant is available.

CML-05 to CML-20 12V / 24V auto Three-stage charging LED display
Phocos · PWM

CA & CM Series

Compact 12V controllers for small loads. The CA06/CA08 add low-battery disconnect and four LED indicators; the CM04/CM10 add no-load disconnect. Pairs with the CMM remote meter.

4 – 10A 12V DC Load disconnect LED charge display
Morningstar · MPPT

SunSaver MPPT

15A MPPT controller that charges 12V or 24V batteries from 24V or 36V arrays, harvesting more energy with up to 97% peak efficiency.

15A MPPT 12V / 24V battery 97% peak efficiency
Morningstar · MPPT

TriStar MPPT

High-current MPPT controllers with four-stage charging for systems up to 3kW, at up to 99% peak efficiency. Available in 30A, 45A and 60A.

30 / 45 / 60A MPPT Up to 3kW 99% peak efficiency
