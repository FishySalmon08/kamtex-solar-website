Solar Panel Mounting Systems

Using quality aluminium and stainless steel, our mounting systems are economical, practical and reliable. The flexibility of the system means it caters to almost all installation types — from tiled roofs to open ground.

Installation types we cover

Tile Roofs

Tile hooks and rails that fix securely without cracking the roof covering.

Flat Roofs

Ballasted and tilted frames that set the optimum angle on flat surfaces.

Metal Seam Roofs

Clamp-on rails for standing-seam metal roofs — no penetration required.

Trapezoidal Roofs

Low-profile mounts fixed to the crests of trapezoidal metal sheeting.

Ground Mount

Freestanding aluminium frames for ground arrays where roof space is limited.

Custom structures

Bespoke frames and poles engineered for jetties, containers and hazardous sites.

Built to last outdoors

Aluminium and stainless-steel components resist corrosion in coastal and industrial environments, so the array stays secure for the life of the system.

  • Corrosion-resistant aluminium and stainless steel
  • Pre-engineered rails and clamps for fast installation
  • Wind and load rated for local conditions

Tell us your roof or ground type and panel count and we will spec the mounting kit.
